Symptoms of Pneumonia

Pneumonia can present with a variety of symptoms, which may vary in severity. Here are some common symptoms to look for:

  • Cough (which may produce phlegm)
  • Fever, sweating, and shaking chills
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest pain when breathing or coughing
  • Fatigue
  • Loss of appetite
  • Confusion, especially in older adults

If you or someone you know is experiencing these symptoms, it is important to seek medical attention promptly.
